ABOUT TECHSPARKS

Our Mission

TechSparks aims to provide a platform for students to showcase their technical skills, foster innovation, and promote a culture of technological advancement. We believe in creating an environment where students can learn, compete, and grow together.

What to Expect

At TechSparks 2025, participants can expect a day filled with exciting technical and non-technical events, networking opportunities, and a chance to win amazing prizes. The event is designed to challenge your skills, expand your knowledge, and provide a fun and engaging experience.

Our History

TechSparks started as a small departmental event in 2020 and has since grown into one of the most anticipated tech events in the region. Over the years, we have seen incredible projects, innovative ideas, and talented participants who have gone on to achieve great things in their careers.
